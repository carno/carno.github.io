Lines of communication and team size

As Mozilla decided to kill Pocket1, I decided to try the link blog approach described by Simon Willison2 to store some of the links I kept in Pocket and revisited on regular basis.

To start with, an informative post by Dave Nicolette on the The LeadingAgile Blog, titled "Lines Of Communication and Team Size: Applying Brooks’ Law"3.

Working in a corporate environment for over 15 years, I have to share or quote it.

Brooks’ Law is real. Be unpredictably rational: Use it.
